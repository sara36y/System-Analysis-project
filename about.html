Car Parking Website

A car parking fringe benefit arises where employer-provided parking facilities are made available to an employee (or associate) and certain additional conditions are satisfied. Car parking benefits provided by small business entities may be exempt from FBT. This exemption has been expanded to businesses with turnover under $50m in relation to benefits provided on or after 1 April 2021.
